Things I can DO with movies

A Combination of Cinema Therapy and CBT techniques, integrated in a Chatbot and developed with Therapists is innovative in the field. Cinema Therapy is a technique that encourages viewers to discover their maladaptive beliefs, problematic thoughts, and feelings while discussing movie characters and stories. Treatment begins with watching a movie and is followed by exercises and video tutorials with the chatbot to track and reflect movie's effect on the viewer.
